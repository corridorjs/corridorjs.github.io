Our Code of Conduct

At CorridorJS all attendees, sponsors, volunteers and staff are required to follow the JSConf Code of Conduct. We take our Code of Conduct very seriously and it will be enforced by organizers during throughout the CorridorJS event, and during our post-event social meetup.
